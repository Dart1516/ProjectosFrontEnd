BUYING IN OUR STORE IS VERY EASY AND SAFE!
- To make purchases on our website, simply choose the product you want and click on "Buy". Some products may vary in color and size. In these cases, you should always select, before clicking buy, the options that best suit your case;
- After choosing the product and clicking on "Buy", you will be directed to the Shopping Cart. Check that the product and the price match the item you have chosen. If you agree, enter your zip code and click "Calculate" to calculate your shipping cost. Choose one of the shipping options and click "Checkout";
- The next step is the identification page, where your registration data will be requested. If this is your first purchase, you will be taken to the registration page. If you are already our customer, enter your login and password and then choose the delivery address for your order;
- Payment is the last step of your purchase. Therefore, check that all your purchase details are correct (products, quantity, price, delivery address). If any information is wrong, change your order.
COMMENTS:
- Credit card purchases are subject to registration verification and document request;
- We do not make shipments to PO Boxes or postage under any circumstances with credit card payments;
- Purchases with third-party cards are subject to delivery address verification. We reserve the right to cancel the order in case of registration discrepancies;
- If you have received a discount coupon, correctly enter the coupon number and click "calculate". The amount will be calculated automatically and added to your purchase. Choose payment method and conditions. You will receive an email confirming all your order information.
Any difficulty or doubt about how to buy in our store, contact the link http://wwwcomputersforyou/contactus
DEADLINES AND DELIVERY
SHIPPING DEADLINES AND FORMS
All products will be shipped according to the way chosen by the customer, very well packed to avoid any kind of damage during transport.
The delivery time is influenced by two factors:
1) According to the shipping method chosen, since the delivery is in charge of the Post Office or Carrier.
2) According to the availability of the product in stock, the postage period (does not consider delivery period) of each product is informed in the availability field, in the announcement of the same.
3) Computers for you does not deliver to PO Box under any circumstances, if this term is not observed, and a PO Box is informed for shipment, the order will be withheld and Customer Service will contact the customer, which may cause delays in shipping depending on the difficulty. of contact.
Example:
Prompt delivery, as soon as your payment is confirmed or the registration verification is completed (when by credit card), the delivery period is up to 3 business days, the delivery period must be considered from that date.
After the post is finished, the code to track your Purchase will be informed through the post office or Carrier website, as soon as it is posted.
What is the deadline for sending (posting) my order?
We always work with products for prompt delivery. The deadline for sending parts orders is up to 3 business days after confirmation of payment, this time is necessary for separation, packaging, invoicing and posting of your order.
PAYMENT
We work with several payment methods
- All payment methods and deadlines do not consider Saturdays, Sundays and holidays. If your purchase is made after 18:00 pm, consider counting the deadline on the next business day.
